Security & Privacy

Privacy-First Architecture

Your academic records are private. We build with security from the ground up.

End-to-End Encryption

All data transit channels are secured with E2E industry-grade SSL encryption protocols, shielding your chat content.

Privacy-First

Choose exactly which group chats or chats you want the AI companion to read. Chatnalyxer will ignore everything else.

No Message Storage

We process messages transiently to extract deadlines. Once tasks are identified, the chat text is deleted permanently from our servers.
