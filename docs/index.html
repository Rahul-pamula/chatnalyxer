How It Works

01

Install & Register

Download the Chatnalyxer app for iOS or Android. Create a secure student profile to house your custom calendar and alerts.

02

Scan Authentication QR

Open WhatsApp on your phone and scan the secure in-app QR code to connect your session to our secure background listener.

03

Configure Active Groups

Toggle and select which specific class group chats, study hubs, or project channels you want Chatnalyxer to monitor.

04

Real-Time AI Filtering

Gemini NLP actively listens to incoming texts, filtering out day-to-day chatter, greeting spam, and messages without tasks.

05

Multi-Modal Processing

Upload a syllabus PDF, snap photos of homework schedules, or forward voice messages. AI parses text, charts, and speech.

06

Verify & Adjust Tasks

Review automatically generated event cards. Tweak titles, due dates, or urgency scores with our built-in AI chat assistant.

07

Universal Calendar Ingestion

Save events to your personal schedule. Sync is pushed instantly to Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, or local memory.

08

Persistent Priority Alerts

Urgent tasks trigger mobile alarms. High-priority deadlines persist in your notifications drawer until marked complete.
